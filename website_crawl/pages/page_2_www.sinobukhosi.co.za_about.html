We are a family owned investment company that exists to create sustainable growth for future generations. Our focus is to strategically allocate capital that drives growth and achieve value creation through long term partnerships. We do this by investing in established businesses poised for growth and in need of a partner to reach the next step in their journey of growth.

VALUES

We look to invest in businesses that have potential to operate for generations to come. Businesses who support economic growth in South African and are key contributors to job creation.

We bring to the table patient capital, corporate experience, skills and knowledge. We have operational experience having been entrepreneurs. If and where necessary, we bring transformation ownership.
